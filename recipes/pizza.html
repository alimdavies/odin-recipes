Pizza Recipe

This is a classic pizza recipe that you can customize with your favorite toppings. It's easy to make at home and perfect for a family meal or gathering.

Ingredients

  • 250g pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200g pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g pepperoni slices (or your choice of toppings)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (428°F). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a baking sheet or a pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the pepperoni slices or your choice of toppings.
  5. Drizzle a tablespoon of olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza onto the stone. If using a baking sheet, simply place it in the oven.
  7. Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Top with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
