Roast Chicken Recipe

This is a simple and delicious recipe for roast chicken that results in a juicy and flavorful bird. Perfect for a family dinner or special occasion.

Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 lemon, halved
- 4 garlic cloves, smashed
- Fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, or parsley)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 onion, quartered
- 2 carrots, cut into chunks
- 2 potatoes, cut into chunks

5. Arrange the quartered onion, carrots, and potatoes around the chicken in the oasting pan. Drizzle with a little more olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
6. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 75째C (165째F) and the juices run clear when pierced at the thigh.
7. Baste the chicken with the pan juices halfway through cooking to keep it moist.
8. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving.
9. Serve the roast chicken with the roasted vegetables on the side.
